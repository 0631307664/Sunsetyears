About Me

Hello, I’m Nish Ramphal, a woman on a mission to inspire and transform the lives of women over 50. Growing up as a brown woman in a third-world country, I’ve seen how patriarchy and limited worldviews can confine women to roles within the home, workplace, and church. But I believe there’s a greater purpose for us all.

That’s why I believe we, as older women, must step up to become the mentors younger women desperately need.

But how can we guide others effectively if we’re not actively growing ourselves? We need to educate ourselves, take care of our physical and mental health, and nurture our spiritual lives.

Growing up, I watched my parents struggle with poverty and limited resources, but they persevered. Now, in our 50s, it’s our time to shine. Let’s break free from societal constraints, heal from past wounds, and step boldly into the life God has planned for us.

My Vision

My heart is for women who have felt trapped, limited, or unseen—especially those navigating the later stages of life. Together, we can break free from societal and personal constraints, embrace our individuality, and step into the plans God has for us. It’s never too late to reinvent yourself, heal from past wounds, and grow closer to God.

Some topics we’ll explore include:

  • Personal growth and self-awareness
  • Physical and mental wellness
  • Faith and spiritual growth
  • Mentoring and empowering younger women
  • Overcoming adversity and finding purpose
  • Bible studies and devotionals
  • Personal anecdotes and lessons learned

Let’s embark on this transformative journey together.
